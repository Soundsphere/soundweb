A spot I found a long time ago, but it only now catches on. Long curbs on all sides with slightly banked areas. Basically a long fronside ledge/curb thingy that is tons of fun.


The closest public transport stop is Kriminalgericht Moabit (M10). But you can easily walk there from the central train station (Hauptbahnhof))
